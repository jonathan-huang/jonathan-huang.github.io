QFT II Final Presentation

My slides

My final presentation is about the study of percolation using field theory approaches, and the main focus is on the 2018 paper The F ield Theory Approach to Percolation Processe by Hans-Karl Janssen and Uwe C. Tauber. I got my idea from population models in mathematical biology, where birth-death phenomena can be simulated on a lattice and formulated in the language of Feynman path integrals an dfield theory. For example, refer to the classical paper by Peliti, Path integral approach to birth-death processes on a lattice.

量子場論 II 期末報告

我的簡報

我的期末報告是有關使用場論方法研究滲流現象（percolation），主要的重點是 Hans-Karl Janssen 和 Uwe C. Tauber 在 2018 年發表的論文 The Field Theory Approach to Percolation Processes。我的想法來自數理生物學中的族群模型，其中生滅現象（birth-death phenomena）可以在晶格上進行模擬，並用費曼路徑積分和場論的語言來表述。例如，可以參考 Peliti 的經典論文 Path integral approach to birth-death processes on a lattice。
