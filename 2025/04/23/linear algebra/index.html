Linear Algebra (Yang-Yi Fang)

The course linear algebra is a two-semester course, and is taught by Professor Yang-Yi Fang in the 2024 Fall and the 2025 Spring terms.

  • Lecturer: Professor Yang, Yi-Fang
  • Textbook: Friedberg, S. H.; Insel, A. J.; Spence, L. E. (2003). Linear Algebra 4e [FIS] (main) and Hoffman, Kenneth M; Kunze, Ray (1971). Linear Algebra 2e [H&K] (supplementary).
  • Difficulty:

Some of the proofs of extra exercises are not complete.
