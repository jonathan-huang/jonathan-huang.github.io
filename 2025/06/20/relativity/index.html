Theory of Relativity

Theory of Relavity (Prof. Chiang, Cheng-Wei)

Lecturer: Prof. Chiang, Cheng-Wei
Grade: A+
Textbook: d’Inverno, Ray; Vickers, James (2022). Introducing Einstein’s Relativity (This book can be downloaded for free on the Internet)

Final Presentation

Our final presentation is based on the paper Am. J. Phys. 92, 878–884 (2024) A simple model of a gravitational lens from geometric optics, which explores the optico-mechanical analogy and general relativity.

In our written report, the historic note section and some of the derivations are done by me. Also, I prepared and delivered the oral presentation, which is presented in Mandarin. The slides template is designed by me, and is free to distribute.
