Theory of Relativity 相對論

Lecturer: Prof. Chiang, Cheng-Wei 蔣正偉老師
Grade: A+
Department: PHYS
Credit(s): 3
Materials: d’Inverno, Ray; Vickers, James (2022). Introducing Einstein’s Relativity (This book can be downloaded for free on the Internet)

Final Presentation

Our final presentation is based on the paper Am. J. Phys. 92, 878–884 (2024) A simple model of a gravitational lens from geometric optics, which explores the optico-mechanical analogy and general relativity.

In our written report, the historic note section and some of the derivations are done by me. Also, I prepared and delivered the oral presentation, which is presented in Mandarin. The slides template is designed by me, and is free to distribute.

期末報告

我們的期末報告是基於 Am. J. Phys. 92, 878–884 (2024) 的論文 A simple model of a gravitational lens from geometric optics，該論文探討了物理學中的光–力類比 (optico-mechanical analogy) 以及廣義相對論。

在我們的書面報告中，我負責歷史背景部分與一些推導。此外，我也準備並負責口頭報告（中文）。歡迎自由使用簡報模板。
