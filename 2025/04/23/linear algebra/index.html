Linear Algebra (Yang, Yi-Fan)

The course Linear Algebra is a two-semester course taught by Professor Yang-Yi Fan in the 2024 Fall and the 2025 Spring semesters.

Lecturer: Professor Yang, Yi-Fan
Grade: A+ / A-
Textbook:

  • Friedberg, S. H.; Insel, A. J.; Spence, L. E. (2003). Linear Algebra 4e [FIS] (main)
  • Hoffman, Kenneth M; Kunze, Ray (1971). Linear Algebra 2e [H&K] (supplementary).

This course is a mandatory course for students of mathematics in National Taiwan University. Some of the proofs of extra exercises are not complete.
